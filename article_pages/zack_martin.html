Zack Martin: America's Lineman

Zack Martin is one of the best offensive linemen in the NFL today and has been a dominant force since he was drafted by the Dallas Cowboys in 2014. Martin has been a consistent performer for the Cowboys and has been a key part of their offensive success over the years.

Martin was an immediate impact player in his rookie season, starting all 16 games and earning a Pro Bowl selection. Since then, he's continued to improve each year and has been named to the Pro Bowl and selected All-Pro in each of his first eight seasons, an astounding feat.

Martin's dominance on the field is due to his exceptional technique and physicality. He has a rare combination of strength, agility, and intelligence, which makes him an excellent pass blocker and run blocker. Martin's ability to anchor the offensive line has been a key factor in the success of the Cowboys' offense.

In addition to his individual accolades, Martin has also been a part of some of the best offensive lines in the league during his time in Dallas. Alongside fellow linemen Tyron Smith and Travis Frederick, Martin helped the Cowboys have one of the best rushing attacks in the NFL, with Ezekiel Elliott rushing for over 1,000 yards in each of his first three seasons in the league.

Martin's dominance and consistency over the years has cemented his status as one of the best offensive linemen in NFL history. He has already earned a place in the Cowboys' Ring of Honor and is well on his way to a Hall of Fame career. The Cowboys hope they can count on Martin to continue to be a key part of their offense for a couple more seasons.
