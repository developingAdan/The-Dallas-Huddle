Stephon Gilmore: A Bigger Contribution?

The Dallas Cowboys made a big move this past offseason, acquiring veteran cornerback Stephon Gilmore in a trade with the Indianapolis Colts. This move signaled the Cowboys' commitment to improving their defense by adding a seasoned leader to the secondary.

Gilmore brings a wealth of experience and talent to the Cowboys' defense. He's a former Defensive Player of the Year and a five-time Pro Bowler. His coverage skills and ability to shut down opposing receivers are well-known around the league.

In Dan Quinn's defense, Gilmore will be a key player in the secondary. His versatility and ability to play both man and zone coverage will allow Quinn to mix up his defensive schemes and confuse opposing quarterbacks. With Gilmore on the field, the Cowboys' defense will have a reliable shutdown corner who can take away one side of the field, while having ball-hawking Trevon Diggs on the other.

While winning the highly-touted NFL's defensive player of the year award (as a CB!) in 2019, it's not just Gilmore's on-field abilities that make him an important addition to the Cowboys. He's also a seasoned veteran with a wealth of knowledge and leadership qualities that will be invaluable to the young Dallas defense. His experience and leadership will help the younger players develop and bloom.

The Cowboys' defense hasn't struggled since the addition of Dan Quinn, and the addition of Gilmore is a sign that the team is serious about improving. With a talented roster on both sides of the ball, the Cowboys have a chance to be a contender in 2023, and Gilmore's presence on the team will only increase their chances of success and will do moving forward.

Overall, the trade for Stephon Gilmore was a major move for the Cowboys, and his impact on the field and in the locker room will be crucial to the team's success in 2023 and beyond. With his leadership and talent, the Cowboys have added a key piece to their defense, and fans can expect big things from Gilmore in a Cowboys uniform.
