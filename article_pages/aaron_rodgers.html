Aaron Rodgers: The Dallas Boogeyman

A aron Rodgers has been one of the most dominant quarterbacks in the NFL over the past decade. Rodgers has been somewhat of a boogeyman for the Cowboys, consistently putting up impressive performances against them in crucial moments.

One of Rodgers' impressive performances was a 34-31 victory in the 2017 Divisional Playoffs. In that game, Rodgers threw for 356 yards and four touchdowns, including a remarkable 36-yard pass to tight end Jared Cook on third-and-20 (!?) in the final minutes of the game to set up the game-winning field goal.

Overall, Rodgers has faced the Cowboys nine times, winning six of those games. He has thrown for over 2,150 yards, 14 touchdowns, 1 interception and has maintained a 103.6 passer rating against them. Just complete domination.

While the Cowboys have had their fair share of success against the Packers over the years, Aaron Rodgers has been a consistent thorn in their side. With Rodgers now in New York, it will be interesting to see if the Cowboys can finally find a way to slow down one of the NFL's all-time great quarterbacks when they play this upcoming season.
